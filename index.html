Any questions? Here’s the answers to the questions we get a lot:

Will I receive the same savings as the examples on this page?

No…but that’s not a bad thing - you could actually save more than the examples here! Every home is different, as is every solar and battery installation. In order to create an accurate saving projection for your home, you will need to book a free solar and battery assessment, with one of our expert team of energy assessors.

Do solar panels work even when the weather is cloudy?

Yes! Solar panels work off of daylight, not just direct sunlight. Typically on a cloudy day, the performance of the solar system is half what you would normally produce on a sunny day. When you get your free quote, we factor weather data into our projections for your average solar generation, so you have a realistic view of how your solar system will perform over the course of the year.

How do solar systems produce electricity to power my home?

The electricity collected from the solar system is converted from direct current (DC) to alternating current (AC) with the help of a solar inverter. Electricity generated by the solar system is first utilised by the house, with any leftover paid back to the grid at a rate decided by your Electricity retailer FIT (Feed In Tariff). If you choose a Social Energy package with Solar Power Nation, you’ll get access to the market leading FIT!3

Do I have to pay for the full cost of the system up front?

No - in fact many of our customers choose to spread the cost, so that they can save as they pay, with the cost of the system offset by the savings that they make. In some cases, the savings generated by the solar, battery and Social Energy system can be greater than the monthly system payment, meaning you can be cash positive with your green investment!

Does a solar system work at night to keep powering my home?

With solar on its own, no. However, we recommend installing a smart battery alongside solar panels, so that you can keep using your own free green power day and night.

Is there any government support available for solar and battery systems?

As Australia goes green, there are some fantastic savings available with government incentives. These vary region to region and are subject to eligibility. To find out what savings you could be eligible for, book a call with our expert team today.
